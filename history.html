The History of Stoke Newington

Stoke Newington is very much an old but always progressive area. Explore it's story below.

  • The Doomsday Book

    1086

    Stoke Newington was mentioned in the Doomsday Book (1086): "It had a recorded population of 41 households in 1086, putting it in the largest 20% of settlements recorded in Domesday" source. The Doomsday Book was a survey of Britain ordered by William the Conqurer, who famously invaded Britain after the Battle of Hastings in 1066.
